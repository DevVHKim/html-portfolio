The Best Movies According to Victor

My top movies of all-time

Serenity - sci-fi

Serenity is a cowboy style sci-fi movie. Based on the TV series, "Firefly" This film takes you on a journey with a crew
aboard the spaceship. They travel across the galaxy to complete their hauling runs, smuggling contraband, anything
to make money to survive.

Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse

This grammy nominated film is an Animated movie from Marvel, of Spiderman. Follows the character Miles Morales
who stumbled upon a glitched, radioactive spider from another universe that bit him. Causing him to obtain spider-like
abilities. With his newfound powers, he is forced to overcome challenges and obstacles
